About Us

Born in January 2005, Micropan Pharmachem Pvt. Ltd. is among the leading service providers of Dry Powder Particle Size Reduction Services called as Micronization of Pharmaceuticals Raw Materials, Analytical Testing Laboratory and PQD Manufacturers across the country. The product range which we use to micronized comprise Metronidazole, Glimepiride Sulfonamide, Sodium Bromide, Divalproex Sodium and many other products. With the continuous hard work of our team and co-operative staff, we have received trust of patrons for ourselves from all over the country.

We have a well developed infrastructure facility for organized execution of all the business activities. We are highly dedicated to fulfill the increasing demand of all quality micronized products by delivering them the best market product at cheapest cost among the all competitors. All our machines are made of pure stainless steel L316 with fine finishing and favor easy cleaning. Our distributive network helps us in meeting the increasing requirements of the clients. We always seek for the best manufacturer of our delivered products. To manage all the procurement activities, we have a team of skilled professionals to ensure the premium quality and safety with the products.

Under the visionary leadership of our mentor, Mr. Atul Pande (Director) we have been able to occupy a well appreciated position in the market. His continuous motivation, extreme guidance and outstanding managerial qualities allow us to gain the trust of huge number of customers.
